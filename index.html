How to Play

1. Use Arrow Keys or WASD to navigate your character (Blue) through the maze.

2. Collect Coins (Yellow) to increase your score.

3. Avoid the Monster (Red) which tracks you using A* Search.

4. Compete against the AI Player (Green) making Minimax decisions.

5. Reach the Exits (Purple) after 15 turns to secure your victory.

6. Press 'R' to reset the board at any time.
